Inktober 2017

Inktober + Malayalam Words + Malayalam Type

Inktober 2017 started around the time we traditionally do Saraswati Puja, which involves writing the Malayalam alphabet in rice early in the morning.

Neither capable of rising early in the morning or of arranging for rice on a plate, I opted to merge the tradition with Inktober and attempted to associate malayalam words somehow associated with the word pf the day, but also starting with the alphabet letters in sequence.
